The Network

Connecting The Nation, a vast store of information and the operating system of mankind

The Network connects all citizens with News, Information, communication and entertainment. Running essential systems, it operates with a degree of sentience and is under the direct influence of The Overseer. Whose Mind is connected to The Network.

Note: Contents of The Network are heavily monitereed and all additions regulated to offical publishers. While sharing simularity's to the Internet of the 21st Century, it is far less chaotic and less able to create social incoahesion.

The Service's of The Network

The Network offers many services to The Nation, limited of course to Approved Content

Entertainment:

Citizens of The Nation have access to a vast ammount of Games, shows and articles etc, to choose from. Citizens can also create their own content through a varity of different means. The Nation provides a varity of tools and the neccessary education to be creative through The Art Union.

News:

The World is connected at all times by 24/7 News, updating The Nation at all times. With specialised programs at 8am and 5pm realtive to each Region's Timezone, with hosts that are incredibly skilled in their proffession.

Scientific Knowelege:

A well informed public is the corner stone of any fuctional Nation. The Network updates a comprehensive guide to the discoverys and to an extent the inner workings of the Science Union. (Approved Content of course)
