"The World"

"Things arent the way they where before, you wouldnt recognise the Earth Anymore" - Morbid Joke among The Knights

Home of The Nation: The Earth in the 22nd Century is a shadow of its former self. Vast dead zones across the Oceans, polluted landscapes and deserts as far as the eye can see. However The Nation is rebuilding. Rebuilding the Ecosystems so they can survive this New Earth, and working hard to make the planet green once more!

Politics of The Nation:

With The Nation organised into 375 Regions, there is political structure in place to ensure human representation across the Earth. Working with other elected members or specailists from organisations such as the Science Union, the representative government ensures Social Cohesion and that all citizens of The Nation have their needs met. All Elected representatives have their identity hidden while in public or parlimentary sessions, to limit the forming of factions, voting blocks or the unlikely chance of outside influence.

Note: Any elected Representative is not above being charged for Treason, deposed or excecutued if deemed neccessary by Command or the Inquistion. Any Behaviour or action that goes against the interest of The Nation will not be taken lightly.

Regional Chancellor:

Elected by the entire Region in a direct election, they lead Federal Parliments. Acting as Speakers, as well as working with the Regions Praetorians, Head Scientists and the Heads of other organisations. Which allows them to form a Regional Mangement Council

Federal MP:

Elected to represent a Federal electorate, which includes a Federal city and its connected State and County urban centers. They work together in their Regional Parliment, meeting in the Regional Capital. Bringing with them their State MP's. There are a total of 10 Federal Members in each Region

State MP:

Elected to represent a State electorate, which includes a State town, and its connected county urban centers. They attend Regional Parliments with their Fedearl Representative. There are a total of 10 State MP's per Federal electorate, making a total of 100 per Region.

Councillor:

Elected to repersent each County urban center, they meet with their fellow Councillors in their State Electorate to work with their State MP. With a total of 10 Councillors per State electorate, makes a total of 1000 Per Region.

Urban Centers

With the Regions of The World, each containing around 4 million citizens. Lets break down the structure of Urban Centres that works alongside the Political system.

Regional Capital's

The Administrative Center for the Region, will often contain headquaters for different organisaions and departmnets. Houses the Regional Parlimentry tower. Often found in the dead center of the Region itself, it can sometimes be found on the sites of former Major City's. The Capitals act as major railway hubs for the Region and nodes for express lines that connect Regional Capitals. Carrying goods as well as people across The Nation.

Population: 1,000,000
Number of: 1
Purpose: Administrative

Federal City's:

These city's are decently sized, and serve as Resource Hubs, with the Resources produced and manifactured from State and County Centers, transported and stored here. Before being moved across the Region or Nation according to need. Federal City's also act as centers of different arts, and educational services. These City's tend to connect directly by rail to the Capitals, each other and their respective State Towns.

Population: 100,000
Number of: 10
Purpose: Resource Storage and Tranfer, Entertainment, Education.

State Towns:

These towns are vitally important for the region, containing industry's that refine raw resources that are pumped into them from surrounding county's. They also contain food production towers, vertical farms to grow the food that feeds The Nation. They act as rail hubs for surrounding county's and connect to each other and their repsective Federal city.

Population: 10,000
Number of: 100
Purpose: Food Growth, Refinement and manafacturing

County's:

The Most numorous and smallest urban centers, they are the bedrock of The Nation. The Place of birth for every citizen in The Nation. County's house The Nations Human Reproductive Centers, where humans are grown in artifical vats before being run in State Education systems. living together till the age of 12 when they start learning to be more independent by living in smaller groups or even alone. The building blocks of social Cohesion are put together here, with the base education centers that will give each citizen the skills to be a happy and productive member of society!

Population: 1,000
Number of: 1000
Purpose: Human Production and Base Education, Mining

Regions of The Nation:

With much of the Earths former coast lines gone, many major city's now lie partially or fully submerged. New Urban centers scattered carefully across the Globe now hold the Population of The Nation. Formed into the 375 Regions. These are the first 10 Regions that where created in the formation of The Nation.

The first Region of The Nation, located in former Queensland, Australia. This Region saw the Birthplace of the Movement, which lead to the creation of The Nation. This Region is represented by the Numeral I, meaning 1

The Secound Region of The Nation, located in roughly News South Wales, Australia. Containing the former capital seized by The Movement, a step towards the creation of The Nation. This Region is represented by the Numeral II, meaning 2. It also offered the greatest resistance to The Movement in its formation, outside of Region I.

The Third Region of The Nation, located in former Victoria, Australia. This was where The Network was first created, on a floating platform just offshore rom former Melbourne. The Rest of the Floating Capital was built around it. This Region is represented by the Numeral III, meaning 3.

The Forth Region of The Nation, located in former Tasmania, Australia. Its unsually small for a Region. A left over from the formation of the First 10 Regions in Australia, being the origin of The Nation. Making it technically one of the most dencely population Regions in the world. This Region is represented by the Numeral IV, meaning 4.

The Fifth Region of The Nation, located in the Easten half of former South Australia, Australia. This Region is represented by the Numeral V, meaning 5.

The Sixth Region of The Nation, located in the Eastern half of the former Northen Territory, Australia. This Region is represented by the Numeral VI, meaning 6.

The Seventh Region of The Nation, located in the Western half of the former Northen Territory, Australia. This Region is represented by the Numeral VII, meaning 7.

The eighth Region of The Nation, located in the western half of former South Australia, Australia. This Region is represented by the Numeral VIII, meaning 8.

The Ninth Region of The Nation, located in the northen half of former Western Australia, Australia. This Region is represented by the Numeral IX, meaning 9.

The Tenth Region of The Nation, located in the southern half of former Western Australia, Australia. This Region is represnted by the Numeral X, meaning 10.
