The Knight Program

Before The Movement, there was The Founders. The Founders became the Knights, The Knights built The Nation.

Forming the Ranks of The Knight Program. Some of the most loyal and ideologically motivated Party Members of The Movement, have survived The Great War and built The Nation for all of humanity.

The Ranking Structure:

Apart of a chain of Command coming from the Overseer himself. The Knights are the finest The Nation has to offer and stand ready to maintain The Nations Goals what ever the cost. Knights operate in Urban Centers, and are decorated in numerals and capes that denote their region of origin and rank. Note: The Chain of command can be overridden by Command at any point if deemed neccessary.

The Overseer

The Original Founder of the Movement himself, he is connected to The Network and Command. Through which he Oversees The Nation. Often taking an indirect role in affairs of State. Able to shut down any Knight's suit to prevent the extremly small chance of insubordination. Residing in The Floating Captial, at its very center.

The Praetorian

Each Region has a Praetorian who overseers all Knights within their Region, in charge of approving recruitment of new knights and meets in The Praetorian Council to dicuss and vote about major National decisions. Each Praetorian was highly experienced and loyal members of The Movement, often coming from high ranking individuals from The Great War. Unlike lower ranks, in peacetime and out of War Game scenarios they still play a vitial role in running The Nation and Maintaining The Knights. Being far more Political in nature, having to work with Elected Regional Chancellors and The Heads of Various Unions, such as the Science or Art Unions.

The Centurions

Under the Praetorians are The Centurions, operating as commanders of around 100 Knights. Centurions often reside in State Town centers where they can quickly travel to visit and oversee the garrisions of surrounding County's. Outside of war games or actual war, they have little to no responsibility's. Their ability's and responsibility's focus on organising Knights and supporting military forces. Working as field commanders for Standard military forces.

Squad Leaders

In charge of about 10 Knights, they lead squads of Knights and are in charge of what training they may require. Often put in charge of directly Training and mentoring New Recruits.

Standard Knights

The bulk of The Knights, many of whom fought in The Great War. Often form garrisions in urban centers, and have no real role outside of military related affairs. They tend to thus blend into the citizen population, with a lot of free time. The Knights are the best The Nation has to offer and the selection process is very strict and carefull. Most citizens do not see Knights close up, or know to much about them and thus their position in Socity is legendary. They are loyal to Command however, and are ready to step in to ensure The Nation remains on course to its goals. Their Suits are incredible peices of Tech. Its Blue Body Suits while offering resistance to incoming fire and physical damage. Contains artifical muscle's that are also capable of self healing and manipulation by the user. A Knight through prolonged training and exposure to A Suit will naturally improve in skill and ability. Their suits adjusting overtime to how the user operates them, becoming more efficent and adapted to The User itself.
