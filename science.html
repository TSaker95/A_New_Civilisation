"The Science Union"

"We are the Universe understanding itself"

After Century's of Ignorance, The Science Union was created to make and maintain the light of knowledge for all of Humanity. Fiercy independent and autonomus, their pusuit of science is almost fanatical in nature. Kept in check only by the Praetorians and The Overseeer himself.

Science Union Division's

Incredibly proud and partly Autonomus, the Science Union is split into multiple divisions. Each based on a respective and broad fields. Which Each Region containing a number of Science Union Facility's. Each divison elects a Head Scientist to represent them, and work with Regional Chancellors and Praetorian's. Holding a very respected position in The Nation's leadership for Each Region.

Note: The Rivially between the division's is encouraged to spur compertion and innovation, while remaining friendly in nature (mostly)

The Bio Division:

Like any Division they are incredibly proud of their work, especially having decoded the building blocks of life itself. Able to manipulate cells and DNA as a programer could write lines of Code. They are repsonsible often for improving existing or creating new life to adapt to the harsh Climate of The 22nd Century Their main rivials are the Robitics Division, whom they compete often with to make better 'creations'. Beliving anything biological is better than anything Robotic. Their feild has lead to the irradication of all disease's and have lead to a carefully created strain of Human, that is both able to survive the Harsh environment and fitter and healthier than any normal human being before The Nation. This of course was limited to ensure that The Nation remains an Empire of Humanity, and it is forbidden to modify a humans mental state. They are responsible for all physical healthcare and the Reproduction of The Human Race, growing new generations of Humans.

The Robotics Division:

Often Rivials of the Bio Division, due to their competing focus on making certain Machines and equipment. Their colaboration has lead to a mix of biological and Robotic techs, as found in the blue scaled skin that can be seen in Knghts Armor and Machines that require moving parts such as Walkers. They often strive to make The Nation more efficent, only held back by the Social Division which deems total automation as a risk to Social Cohesion. They can be incredibly adapatable at figuring out tech and how it works.

The Social Division:

No Society can be built to last without propor social plannings. This Division carefully study's Humanity itself and constantly works across The Nation to ensure and maintain Social Cohesion. Thus they have influenced how City's are designed and Education Systems operate. Thus The Nation is able to maintain A unifying and Cohesive Culture. They are frequiently at odds with other divisions of the Science Union, because they step in and add a new layer of thinking that more straight forward logical thinkers would not consider. The Importance of how human's think and operate is always thus taken into consideration, much to the dismay of their peers.

The Physics Division:

In charge of exploring the building blocks of the universe and the limits of reality, unlike the other divisions they push into the theoretical at a greater extreme. Working on the Technology that allows for the opening between Reality's: The Creation of The Multiverse Portal's They have little rivialy or even contact with other divisions as their feild is often self contained in nature.

The Astro-Geo Division:

Having no rivials for obvious reasons, they study Geography on Earth and on planets and moons across the Solar System. Identifying sites across the land and ocean floor sutible for minning and building if necessary. They often work with The Bio Division to ensure the introduction of new species in appropriate areas. Often made fun of by other Divisions for being boring and to "down to earth" in nature. They often push for manned missions across the Solar System, but Command has other plans.

Science Union Reports:

With Different Branchs of The Science Union, focusing on their respective feilds. Prominent Scientists often submit reports to Command, before less sensitive data is able to be published to The Network to be read by the Public.
