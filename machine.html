Machines Of The Nation

"The Technology of The Nation, has allowed for the creation of Machines of incredible power and grace"

The Nation has the Technological and Industrial power to create machines to stock the world with a powerful military capacity.

Machines:

With The Regions of The Nation, competing in yearly Wargames. Each are assigned a number of War Machines to test and train in.

A Heavy class of machine, the tank can be used for a varity of roles with a great deal of potential if used correctly. Each Region should have a balanced stock of tanks available to them, this machine is available and adviced to be used wisely. The Weight of this Tank is supported by Anti Grav tech, allowing a heavier armored vehicle that is able to be both agile and fast.

An all rounder Class of Gunship. Fairly well armoured and armed, it can move with a moderate speed while laying down impressive fireapower includes limited transportation capacity of cargo. Not able to be used to transport squads.

Designed for defensive emplacements, or as a semi mobile firing platform. The Arti-gun comes in different variations, this one being designed to employ anti tank/knight firepower

Hiding in the depths bellow is an underwater predator. Fast, manverable and powerful, it will make any battle at sea a challenge for an opponent. Able to be used as a surprise missle platform, a strike craft or even a scout.

The Sheppard Class Walker. Originally created as a last resort weapon, and a defense against incoming Meteorite's. It is nicknamed: The City Killer, for its ability to take out city's with one shot.
