The Manifesto:

Forging A New Civilisation

1. “There is no race but The Human Race.” All race-based politics and dialogue must end. All genders are equal and there is to be no discrimination between them. Segregation in any form cannot exist between Human beings, and it will be ensured that they never will again.

The world mapped out into regions according to population centres, to ensure a democratic system shall represent all people across the Earth.

3. “All cultures and languages must become one”. To break down all divides, One culture shall be formed that upholds the ethics of a nation for humanity. There is no gods and all religions are false. To believe otherwise is to hold back and divide humanity. All faith’s must only become myth, places of worship converted or dismantled.

Each person will have their physical, emotional, mental needs met, their wants met within reason provided they do not impact the rights of another individual. Phycological care, will be mandatory.

5. "Sustainability as a civilisation is the most important goal of any society, to do otherwise will lead to its inevitable destruction." The Earth is finite and must be protected at all costs; Damage to the earth will be undone, and ecosystems protected from harm.

6. "Education will be free and required by all people to ensure an educated civilisation." This includes any education a citizen may demand or require. We must ensure everyone can understand the nature of reality, through the creation of A Science Union.

7. "All cities must densify, and urban sprawl be abandoned." City’s shall become regulated in size, to limit environmental impact on one given area. Population will be sorted as to ensure a healthy ecosystem, as well as physical, social and phycological health of all citizens.

8. "Birth is to be no longer the Burden of one gender." This biological inequality shall be removed through the process of artificial births. To provide population sustainability, sustainable population growth and to remove the divides between the genders.

9. "Any society, culture or peoples that do not agree must be convinced, transformed or destroyed." There can be no divide in humanity that will allow for long term disunity.

10. "Those Responsible for Climate change will be judged based on their knoewledge, ability and unwillingness to take action." The Guilty Shall be Slaughtered, and their names remembered as the villians of History. Humanity will hate them, and they will suffer for their crimes against the Earth
